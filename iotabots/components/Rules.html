Rules

  1. Each player must connect their wallet to the IOTABOTVERSE platform and hold an IOTABOT (ERC721 Token) to participate in the game.

  2. Players can only play against their own IOTABOT.

  3. Each game round consists of one move: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.

  4. To initiate a game, the player must submit a move by signing a transaction on the ShimmerEVM network.

  5. Once both moves have been submitted, the smart contract will determine the winner using the standard Rock Paper Scissors rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.

  6. If the player wins, they are awarded 10 EGGS tokens.

  7. In case of a tie or a loss, the game is declared a draw or a loss respectively, and no rewards will be distributed.
